Point of View

It’s difficult for individuals to remember and document all the places they’ve gone and where they want to go while traveling. Through effective personal categorization, individuals can easily look back at relevant information of these destinations, meaningful memories and experiences.

Contributions: I worked in a team of three. My role was to work through the user interaction of the app, develop screens, and come up with the branding and visual guidelines.

    Final Product

    For our final product, we chose to incorporate these main features:

  • Create & maintain hashtag lists that categorize saved locations

  • Write personal notes for location entry

  • Add unique locations not established on certain maps

  • Take pictures and store it in saved location

  • Create personal and customizable names for lists
